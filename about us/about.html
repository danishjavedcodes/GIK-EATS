Get To Know GikiEats

Order food and online with the GikiEats app

Even when on the move, the free GikiEats mobile application for iOS, Android and Windows Phone, allows you to order food online anytime from anywhere. In three words, the process of ordering food is simple, fast, and convenient.

How to Order

To order food delivery in GIK institute , follow these simple steps:

Enter your delivery address in the location form to see all the places that deliver to your location. It can be your home, office, a hotel or even parks! Choose your dishes. Browse the menu, select your dishes and add them to your basket. When you are done, press the "Review Payment & Address" button. Checkout & Payment. Check your order, payment method selection and exact delivery address. Simply follow the checkout instructions from there. Delivery. We will send you an email and SMS confirming your order and delivery time. Sit back, relax and wait for piping hot food to be conveniently delivered to you!.
